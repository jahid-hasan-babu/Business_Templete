Black Entrepreneur Program

The Black Entrepreneur Program is a partnership between the Government of Canada, Black-led business organizations, and several top financial institutions

The Program will provide loans of up to $250,000 to Black Business Owners and entrepreneurs across the country.

Just contact us and we will help you to prepare and submit your application to the Black Entrepreneur Program.
